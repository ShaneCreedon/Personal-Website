About me

My Story

Hi, I'm Shane, a 23 year old software engineer from Dublin, Ireland. I Studied B.Sc in Computer Applications and Software engineering (DC121) in DCU (Dublin City University) and currently work at Brightflag. I've always had an interest in technology. Questions about how technology worked, why it worked, and how it has changed the world certainly had peaked my curiosity. As a result of this interest, I started programming in small doses and began to involve myself in the software community in general. School never gave me the knowledge to understand how technology in general worked so I decided to pursue this interest and enroll in a software degree. Four years later, I've graduated with a first-class honors degree and still have an incredible passion for software development.

My Hobbies and Interests

Like most children growing up during the 1990s and early 2000s, video games were a big part of my life. Upon playing my very first game, I felt an immediate attachment and love for it. Gaming was the primary reason why I had a curiosity about Computer Science. Similarly, music has also been a passion of mine. I play guitar on a regular basis, mostly riffs and pieces from rock / metal songs that I enjoy. Being creative with programming and working on side-projects is certainly a passion. I also love contributing to Open Source in my spare time.

My Aspirations and Career Goals

I want to grow in all aspects of the software engineering field. Whether that be improving my own engineering ability or simply improving my soft skills. I want to be the best I can at my job and meet great people along the way. I also want to work for a company that which I care about the work they do and strive to pursue each and every day. Alternatively, setting up my own business is another ambition I hope to realise one day, but until that day, Let's see where the wind takes me.
